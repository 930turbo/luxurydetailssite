FAQ

Common questions about vinyl wraps and our studio process.

How long does a full color change take?

Typical full wraps are 3–5 days including prep, install, post-heat, and QC. Show-car or door-returns add time.

Do you cut on the paint?

No. We template, pre-trim, use knifeless tape where needed, and wrap edges when feasible.

How long will it last?

Most premium films are rated 5–7 years on vertical surfaces, less on horizontal. Garage-kept and coated wraps last longer.

Can it be removed?

Yes—proper install means clean removal with heat, leaving OEM paint protected underneath.

Care & Maintenance

Let it cure 24 hours. Hand-wash with pH-neutral soap, no harsh chemicals. Microfiber only. Avoid automatic brushes.

  • Use a dedicated wrap detailer or ceramic specifically for vinyl.
  • Bug/tar: soften with warm water and a gentle citrus-safe product.
  • Avoid pressure-washing close-up on edges and seams.
