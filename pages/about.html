ABOUT LUXURY DETAILS

HISTORY &
EVOLUTION

Founded in 2005 in Framingham, Massachusetts, Luxury Details began as a small audio installation shop built on high quality installs of the leading brands. That reputation quickly fueled growth and led to the move into a larger Southborough facility, where the company operates today.

Over the years, Luxury Details has expanded into a full-service automotive enhancement studio. The team now provides custom audio, radar and laser systems, remote starters, tinting, wraps, paint protection film, lighting, wheels, suspension, detailing, and more—all with the same meticulous attention to detail that defined the shop from the beginning and holds true to the Luxury name.

As an authorized dealer for brands like Alpine, JL Audio, Focal, K40 Radar, and Escort, Luxury Details combines industry-leading products with over two decades of expertise. Every project, whether a simple upgrade or a complete transformation, is approached with professionalism, care, and passion.

Inside Our Process

The Details That Matter

01

Consult

Every project begins with a one-on-one consultation. We take the time to understand your goals, your vehicle, and your vision, ensuring the plan is tailored to both your needs and expectations.

02

Once we understand your vision, we define the exact specifications—selecting the right products, materials, and methods to ensure every detail aligns with performance, style, and long-term reliability.

03

Install

Our team carries out the installation with precision and care, using proven techniques and meticulous craftsmanship. Every component is fitted cleanly and securely, ensuring seamless integration and lasting performance.
